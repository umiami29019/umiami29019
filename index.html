My favorite place I went to before this year was easily Japan. Japan is so beautiful, clean, and modern. The food is amazing (I'm a big fan of ramen noodles), the beef is also great, everything including the subway stations are clean, and the city is so pretty at night with so many different things to see.

My favorite place I went to this year was Prague. Prague was the best city I traveled to in Europe - the food is good, the beer is cheep, the architecture is so beautiful (I was told it avoided WWII bombings and their historical architecture is in tact), and the city was very walkable. I was able to explore most of Prague and I went up to towers for stunning views of the city (all orange roofs and spikes).
